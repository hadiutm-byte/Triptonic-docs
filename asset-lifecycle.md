# Triptonic — Asset Lifecycle

## 1. Purpose

This document defines the lifecycle of a vehicle asset inside Triptonic.

---

## 2. Asset Lifecycle Flow

Acquire → Hold → Lease → Move Market → Sell → Exit

---

## 3. Stages

### 1. Acquire
Asset enters the system.

### 2. Hold
Asset is registered, verified, and evaluated.

### 3. Lease
Asset generates short-term yield.

### 4. Move Market
Asset is rerouted to a higher-opportunity market.

### 5. Sell
Asset is sold at optimized timing and price.

### 6. Exit
Asset leaves the system and performance is recorded.

---

## 4. Decision Rule

At each stage, the system must determine the best next action based on:
- demand
- margin
- market conditions
- risk
- time

---

## 5. Goal

Maximize total asset yield across its full lifecycle.
