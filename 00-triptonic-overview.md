# Triptonic Overview

## What is Triptonic
Triptonic is a vehicle monetization system designed to optimize asset utilization, revenue generation, and operational efficiency for vehicle owners and fleet operators.

It is NOT a marketplace.
It is an intelligent system that enables:
- Vehicle monetization
- Dynamic pricing
- Operational automation
- Data-driven decision making

---

## Core Philosophy
Triptonic transforms vehicles from passive assets into active revenue-generating units.
