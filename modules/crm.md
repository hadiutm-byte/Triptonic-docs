# Triptonic — CRM Module

## 1. Purpose

The CRM Module manages all clients, leads, and interactions.

It is the entry point of revenue into Triptonic.

---

## 2. Core Principle

No client = no deal.

Every deal must originate from a client.

---

## 3. Client Structure

Each client contains:

- Client ID  
- Name / Company  
- Contact details  
- Client type (new / repeat / VIP)  
- Preferences  
- History (past deals)  
- Communication log  

---

## 4. Lead Management

System must capture:

- inbound requests  
- outbound leads  
- referrals  
- repeat clients  

Each lead must:
- be tracked  
- be assigned  
- be converted into a deal  

---

## 5. Sales Workflow

CRM must allow:

1. Lead captured  
2. Client created / matched  
3. Deal created (sent to Deal Engine)  
4. Follow-up tracking  
5. Conversion tracking  

---

## 6. Client Intelligence

System should track:

- booking behavior  
- preferred vehicles  
- price sensitivity  
- frequency  

---

## 7. Sales Control

- each lead assigned to a sales owner  
- no lost leads  
- full visibility on pipeline  
- follow-ups enforced  

---

## 8. Integration

CRM connects to:

- Deal Engine → creates deals  
- Pricing Engine → client pricing logic  
- Inventory → preferences  
- Finance → client payments  

---

## 9. Output

- client database  
- active leads  
- conversion rates  
- sales pipeline  

---

## 10. Goal

Maximize:

- lead conversion  
- client retention  
- revenue growth  

This is your sales engine
