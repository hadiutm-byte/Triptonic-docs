# Triptonic — Finance Module

## 1. Purpose

The Finance Module manages all financial transactions.

It controls revenue, payments, payouts, and profitability.

---

## 2. Core Principle

No payment = no execution.

All money must be tracked, verified, and controlled.

---

## 3. Financial Flow

Each deal must follow:

1. Invoice Generated  
2. Client Payment Received  
3. Payment Confirmed  
4. Operator Cost Recorded  
5. Supplier Paid  
6. Margin Calculated  
7. Deal Settled  

---

## 4. Revenue Structure

Each deal includes:

- Total Revenue (client payment)  
- Total Cost (supplier + ops)  
- Net Profit  
- Margin %  

---

## 5. Payment Control

System must:

- confirm incoming payments  
- link payments to deal ID  
- prevent execution without payment  
- log all transactions  

---

## 6. Payout Logic

- supplier paid ONLY after payment received  
- payout must match agreed cost  
- all payouts logged  

---

## 7. Financial Tracking

System tracks:

- revenue per deal  
- profit per deal  
- total revenue  
- total profit  
- outstanding payments  

---

## 8. Integration

Finance connects to:

- Deal Engine → deal status  
- Pricing Engine → margin  
- Operations → execution confirmation  
- CRM → client payments  

---

## 9. Output

- invoices  
- payment confirmations  
- financial reports  
- profit tracking  

---

## 10. Goal

Ensure:

- full financial control  
- accurate profit tracking  
- secure transactions  

This is your money control system
