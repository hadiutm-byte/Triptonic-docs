# Triptonic — Sales Dashboard (Internal CRM UI)

## 1. Purpose

This is the internal system used by sales teams.

It allows agents to manage clients, deals, and bookings efficiently.

---

## 2. Dashboard Overview

Main sections:

- Leads  
- Active Deals  
- Bookings  
- Clients  
- Revenue  

---

## 3. Lead Management

Sales can:

- view incoming leads  
- assign leads  
- convert leads into deals  

Each lead shows:
- client info  
- request details  
- status  

---

## 4. Deal Management

Sales can:

- create deal  
- update deal status  
- generate quote  
- send to client  

Each deal shows:
- client  
- pricing  
- status  
- timeline  

---

## 5. Quote Management

Sales can:

- generate quotes  
- edit pricing (with rules)  
- send to client  
- track approval  

---

## 6. Booking Management

Sales can:

- view confirmed bookings  
- track execution  
- assist operations  

---

## 7. Client Management

Sales can:

- view client history  
- see preferences  
- manage relationships  

---

## 8. Revenue Tracking

Dashboard shows:

- revenue per deal  
- total revenue  
- profit margins  
- performance  

---

## 9. Alerts & Actions

System must notify:

- new lead  
- deal approved  
- payment received  
- issues in execution  

---

## 10. Integration

Dashboard connects to:

- CRM  
- Deal Engine  
- Pricing  
- Inventory  
- Operations  
- Finance  

---

## 11. UX Principles

- fast  
- clear  
- actionable  
- real-time  

---

## 12. Goal

Enable:

- fast deal conversion  
- full control  
- high efficiency
