# Triptonic — Product (Client Website)

## 1. Purpose

This defines the client-facing website and booking experience.

The goal is to convert visitors into paying customers.

---

## 2. Core Experience

User journey:

1. Land on homepage  
2. Enter trip request  
3. View available vehicles  
4. Receive pricing  
5. Confirm booking  
6. Pay  
7. Track trip  

---

## 3. Homepage (Landing)

Must include:

- luxury visual (cars / lifestyle)  
- strong headline  
- quick booking form  
- trust elements (VIP, reliability)  
- call to action  

---

## 4. Booking Form

Inputs:

- pickup location  
- drop-off location  
- date & time  
- vehicle type  
- special request  

CTA:
→ “Search Vehicles”

---

## 5. Search Results Page

User sees:

- list of vehicles  
- price per option  
- ETA  
- vehicle details  

Actions:

- select vehicle  
- request custom option  

---

## 6. Quote Page

User sees:

- selected vehicle  
- full price breakdown  
- trip details  

Actions:

- confirm booking  
- modify request  

---

## 7. Payment Page

User:

- sees invoice  
- selects payment method  
- confirms payment  

---

## 8. Booking Confirmation

System shows:

- booking ID  
- vehicle details  
- timeline  
- contact support  

---

## 9. Client Dashboard

Client can:

- track booking  
- view past trips  
- manage profile  
- repeat booking  

---

## 10. UX Principles

- fast  
- simple  
- premium feel  
- mobile-first  
- minimal friction  

---

## 11. Integration

Frontend connects to:

- Deal Engine  
- Pricing Engine  
- Inventory  
- Finance  

---

## 12. Goal

Maximize:

- conversion  
- user experience  
- revenue  

This is the revenue interface of Triptoni
